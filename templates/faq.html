Technical Information

Watch for these warning signs:
  • Door becomes unusually heavy and difficult to open manually
  • Door opens partially or very slowly
  • Door drops quickly when partially lifted
  • Springs show visible rust or corrosion
  • Loud bang sounds due to springs unwinding quickly
Never attempt to repair springs yourself - they are under high tension and can be dangerous.

Consider cable repair if you notice:
  • Fraying or rust on cables
  • Slack or loose cables
  • Uneven door movement or shaking
  • Door misalignment
  • Door won't open or close properly
Cables should always be replaced in pairs to ensure proper door balance.

Regular maintenance should include:
  • Lubrication of moving parts
  • Inspection of springs, cables, and rollers
  • Balance and alignment checks
  • Safety sensor testing
  • Hardware tightening
We recommend annual professional maintenance to prevent costly repairs and ensure safe operation.
